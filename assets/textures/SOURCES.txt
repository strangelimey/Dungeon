all licensed CC0 (public domain — no attribution required, recorded here for
provenance). Imported at 1K via `AssetBaker import` (AO baked into albedo,
displacement packed into the normal map alpha).

Higher resolutions (2K/4K/8K) of the same sets are available from Poly Haven
